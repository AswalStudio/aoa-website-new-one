Cancellation & Refund Policy

Official Legal Document

At our company, we strive to provide high-quality digital solutions and personalized services. Because our business model involves the delivery of intangible goods and customized service setups, we adhere to the following policy:

1. No Cancellations

Due to the nature of our services, we do not offer cancellations once an order has been placed.

  • Intangible/Digital Goods: Many of our services are delivered as soft copies or digital assets. Once access is granted or the file is dispatched, the service is considered "consumed."
  • Immediate Processing: To ensure fast delivery, our team begins work on your order immediately upon receipt.

2. No Refunds

All sales are final. We do not offer refunds for any services or products purchased through our website.

  • White-Labeling: Our services often require significant "white-labeling" and custom configuration specifically for your brand. This process involves dedicated labor and resources that cannot be recovered or reused for other clients.
  • Digital Nature: As our products are non-tangible and irrevocable, we do not issue refunds once the order is processed or the digital product is delivered.

3. Quality Assurance

While we do not offer refunds, your satisfaction is important to us. If you believe there is a technical error or the service delivered does not match the specifications agreed upon at the time of purchase:

  • Please contact our Customer Service team within 24 hours of receiving your service or product.
  • Provide a detailed description of the issue.
  • Our management team will review the case and, at its sole discretion, may offer a correction or revision to ensure the deliverable meets the original project scope.

4. Third-Party Costs

In cases where our service involves third-party licenses or vendor tools integrated on your behalf, these costs are non-refundable under any circumstances as they are paid out to external entities.
