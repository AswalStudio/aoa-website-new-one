THE ORIGINAL OLYMPIAD

Where Competition Meets Celebration. Where Every Child Evolves.

Our Genesis

The journey began in 2004. Originally a testing ground for young minds, true evolution requires change. By its fifth year, under the visionary leadership of Jay Vardhan Rishi, the event emerged with a new, joyful identity: The Abacus Olympiad.

From "Battleground" to "Betterment"

“How can we subject a 4-year-old to the ruthless stress of winning and losing? True education should build confidence, not anxiety.”

We refused to let the spirit of learning be crushed by the pressure of ranking. Our purpose shifted from identifying a single winner to fostering a community. We turned the arena of stress into a sanctuary of joy!

The Science of Fun

We do not just count correct answers; we measure cognitive evolution. Our proprietary 5-Point Grid assesses:

  • Tool Maneuvering
  • Visualization
  • Accuracy
  • Speed
  • Mental Stamina
