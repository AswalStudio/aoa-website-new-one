Year 1 Curriculum • 150+ Attachments • By Jay Vardhan Rishi

Year 1 Training is Crucial

The Abacus Olympic Association highly recommends a 5-year program for students from UKG to 5th Standard.

This exclusive, one-of-a-kind exercise curriculum approach handles all the complexities of running Abacus classes in schools. It is designed to easily accommodate challenges, bridging the gap between the unique school ecosystem and traditional private after-school classes.
